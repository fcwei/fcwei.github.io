About

I am a PhD candidate at Tsinghua University. My research lies in theoretical quantum information, especially quantum computation, noise effects, quantum error correction, and nonstabilizerness / quantum magic. I am interested in the structure of noisy quantum systems and the interplay between quantum resources, complexity, and fault tolerance.
